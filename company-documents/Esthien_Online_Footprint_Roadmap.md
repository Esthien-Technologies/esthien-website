# Esthien Online Footprint Roadmap

Prepared: 29 June 2026

## Goal

Make Esthien easier to discover for searches around Esthien, Esthien Labs, Esthien Pvt Ltd, Esthien PVT LTD, Esthien Private Limited, FPGA chipsets, FPGA-first custom silicon, FPGA-class mobile-grade chipsets, bionic arms, prosthetics electronics, edge AI hardware, automotive radar, and physical intelligence.

No one can honestly guarantee a top Google ranking. The practical goal is to make the website authoritative, crawlable, technically fast, and supported by real third-party references. Avoid hidden keyword stuffing because it can hurt ranking and trust.

## 0-30 Days: Foundation

1. Use one public brand and registered name consistently: Esthien Labs as the public brand and Esthien Pvt Ltd as the registered name.
2. Add the confirmed legal name, CIN, registered office, founder names, and official contact email once available.
3. Publish the final domain as `https://www.esthien.com/` and keep all sitemap/canonical URLs consistent.
4. Submit `sitemap.xml` in Google Search Console and Bing Webmaster Tools.
5. Set up Google Analytics, Cloudflare Web Analytics, or privacy-friendly analytics with conversion events for contact clicks.
6. Create official profiles on LinkedIn, X/Twitter, YouTube, GitHub, Crunchbase, Startup India, Wellfound, and relevant deep-tech directories.
7. Publish a brand kit page or downloadable press kit with logo files, company description, founder bio, and approved tagline.

## 30-60 Days: Authority Content

1. Publish 4 technical articles:
   - Why FPGA-based edge AI matters for physical intelligence.
   - FPGA-class mobile-grade chipsets for low-latency embedded control.
   - Bionic arm control architecture: sensing, fusion, and deterministic response.
   - Safety-critical embedded systems for radar, ADAS, and medical assistive devices.
2. Add 2 downloadable PDFs:
   - Company profile one-pager.
   - Technical capability brief.
3. Add structured FAQ content using natural language, not repeated keyword lists.
4. Add founder/company story posts on LinkedIn and link back to the website.
5. Create a GitHub organization with public repositories for non-sensitive demos, documentation, diagrams, or open-source utilities.

## 60-120 Days: Market Signals

1. Seek backlinks from incubators, universities, accelerators, investor portfolio pages, competition pages, partner pages, and technical community pages.
2. Submit to relevant startup, semiconductor, robotics, prosthetics, medical-device, and automotive technology directories.
3. Publish one technical visual per week: block diagrams, architecture notes, explainers, lab notes, or test updates.
4. Apply for credible programs and listings: Startup India recognition, semiconductor or robotics grants, deep-tech challenges, university innovation programs, and accelerator demo days.
5. Create short videos showing engineering progress, founder explanations, and capability demos.
6. Maintain a press/contact page with approved boilerplate, screenshots, and media contacts.

## 120-180 Days: Trust And Conversion

1. Add case-study style pages when there are prototypes, pilots, partners, or validated lab results.
2. Add careers/internship pages with clear roles; recruiting pages create legitimate search footprint.
3. Publish investor-facing updates: milestones, technical roadmap, hiring, partnerships, and prototype progress.
4. Build an email newsletter for monthly technical updates.
5. Track search queries in Search Console and add helpful content for real queries that are getting impressions.
6. Refresh homepage copy every quarter only when the company has new facts, not for keyword churn.

## Website Checklist

1. Keep `title`, meta description, canonical URL, Open Graph tags, sitemap, `robots.txt`, and `llms.txt` current.
2. Keep visible crawlable copy for company aliases and core technologies.
3. Use schema.org Organization and WebSite data.
4. Keep Core Web Vitals strong: avoid heavy initial JavaScript, lazy-load 3D, compress media, and inline critical CSS.
5. Add dedicated pages later for:
   - FPGA chipsets
   - Physical intelligence
   - Bionic arm control systems
   - Prosthetics electronics
   - Automotive radar and ADAS
   - Safety-critical embedded systems

## Monthly Metrics

1. Branded search impressions: Esthien, Esthien Labs, Esthien Private Limited.
2. Non-branded impressions: FPGA chipsets, bionic arm electronics, physical intelligence, edge AI hardware.
3. Organic clicks to homepage and capability pages.
4. Referring domains and backlink quality.
5. Contact button clicks and email inquiries.
6. LinkedIn followers, post reach, and profile visits.
7. Download counts for company profile and technical briefs.
